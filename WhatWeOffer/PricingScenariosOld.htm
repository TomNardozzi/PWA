Sample Pricing Scenarios

Scenario 1

 

Profile: Asset accumulation phase of life, few assets, little need for complex estate planning, several years until retirement, either renting or owning small home.

Plan complexity: Low

Assets: $5,000 in 401k, $5,000 in savings and investments.

 

One-Time Charges

Typical Plan Generation: $150 / hour * 6-10 hours = $900-$1500

Tax Prep Setup: $49

Total One-Time Charge: $949-$1549

 

Annual Charges

Asset Management fee: $10,000 * 0.92% = $92

Decision Support / Review fee: $920

Tax Prep Fee (1040 + 1 state) = $60

Total Annual Charge: $1072 (billed as $253 per qtr + $60 at tax time)
