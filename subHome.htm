Our Mission

At PWA, our mission is to help our clients build and preserve the comfort and freedom of financial independence.  By financial independence, we don't mean solely enabling retirement, like many of the financial advising commercials you'll see on TV.  That's partly because when looking for clients, we don't just seek the elderly couple with a million dollars or more to invest.  On the contrary, we're willing to invest time and energy in our clients to help them become more than an elderly couple that can afford retirement.  Our goal is to get you to "pretirement", a PWA-created term defining the stage of life where your current and future needs are securely fulfilled and you’re financially free to focus on achieving your wants and enjoying life.  Pretirement has nothing to do with being old, tired, and sick of work.  It's about the freedom to do what you've always wanted to do.  Maybe it's owning your own business, writing a book, establishing a private foundation, repairing houses for profit, or doing charity work.  Whatever it is, in pretirement, you can do it on your terms, your schedule, and prioritize it around the rest of your life.  We want to get you there and keep you there, regardless of where you're starting from now.
